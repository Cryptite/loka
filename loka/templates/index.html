Know the Rules

Thievery and No Chest Protection
It can be a tough world out there.

Loka allows thievery and murder, but griefing and mass block damage are forbidden here.

Loka's thieving community is one of the most skilled in Minecraft. Do you challenge them, join them, or ignore them? Some have thrived through conflict, others have fallen. How will your story on Loka be told?

Rich Story
Loka's story is rich and diverse. Will you become part of it?

Since the founding of the server, Lokans have been no strangers to evil, catastrophe, and mystery.

From the feared World-Ender Preksak to the seemingly benevolent god of Asgard, Freyjia, a small host of characters appear from time to time to alter the course of Loka's History. Some players have even chosen to participate and have their roles locked in lore for eternity!
