Our Town System

Discover Loka's Unique Town System!


Loka's towns feature a one-of-a-kind generator system. Through custom code, Loka gives you a unique township experience with control over your town you won't find anywhere else.

Town Generators View the Towns of Loka!

Real Resources

Town generators consume the real-world resource, Emeralds. Through whatever means necessary, you can ensure that your generator will keep running while you're away. Should your generator ever run out of emeralds, though, your protection will fall and thieves will be free to make their way to your vaults.

Town Benefits

Nether Protection

By paying an additional cost, your town can activate nether protection that protects the equivalent sector of the nether for your town. This grants your town an extra layer of security and prevents a variety of nether-entry methods.

Town Chat

Being in a town grants you access to a members-only town chat. Discuss all of your incredibly secret battle plans, or perhaps your odd town's religious rituals. The privacy is yours to exploit!

Town Portal

Town owners may set up a town portal that provides town members a way to warp back to the server spawn. Because warping and other teleports are extremely rare on Loka, town portals grant members quick travel back to spawn

Rank-based Build Priviledges

Bubble generators allow town owners to define regions within their own town borders where only members with a specific rank may build. This allows towns to define districts where veteran town members can build without worrying about new members undoing their fine work.
