Town Generators

Towns

Loka's towns feature a one-of-a-kind generator system. Through custom code, Loka gives you a unique township experience.

Real Resources

Town generator protection runs off of the real-world resource, Emeralds. Either by finding, trading, or converting ores to emeralds with our Ore Converter, you can ensure that your generator will keep running while you're away. If your generator should ever run out of emeralds, though, your protection will fall and thieves will be free to make their way to your vaults.

Town Benefits

Town Chat

Being in a town grants you access to a members-only town chat. Discuss all of your incredibly secret battle plans, or perhaps your odd town's religious rituals. The privacy is yours to exploit!

Town Portal

Town owners may set up a town portal that provides town members a way to warp back to the server spawn. Because warping and other teleports are extremely rare on Loka, these are useful to have if you need to get back to spawn to convert your hard-earned ores to emeralds or take place in some of Loka's more social events like our bi-weekly Auction!

Nether Protection

By paying an additional cost, your town can activate nether protection that protects the equivalent sector of the nether for your town. This grants your town an extra layer of security and prevents a variety of nether-entry methods.
