Loka's towns feature a one-of-a-kind generator system. Through custom code, Loka gives you a unique township experience with control over your town you won't find anywhere else.

Town Generators View the Towns of Loka!

Real Resources

Town generators consume the real-world resource, Emeralds. Through whatever means necessary, you can ensure that your generator will keep running while you're away. Should your generator ever run out of emeralds, though, your protection will fall and thieves will be free to make their way to your vaults.

Town Mastery

25 Town Levels, 18 leveling categories

Almost everything you do counts to the progression of your town. Every block you break or place, every potion you brew, every PvP match you play, every item you sell on the market, every move you make; it all counts!.
