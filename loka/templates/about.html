All About Loka

Everything you need to know about Loka

Give me the rundown, quick.

What you need to know:

  • We are Semi-vanilla - We use custom code to achieve incredible things, but we strive to keep gameplay as close to regular Minecraft as we can.

  • We are a PvP server - Killing, murdering, and any other such pvp method aside from camping are allowed on Loka.

  • We have Towns, but not those towns - We don't run Towny, Factions, or any known town plugin. Our town system is 100% unique and custom to Loka.

  • No Chest Protection here - Hide your things well, we employ no chest protection plugins.

  • Steal Away! - Thievery, stealing, coersion, manipulation. These are all allowed and found on Loka. Keep your friends close and your enemies closer, life can be very hard. There are limits, though; more about that later.

  • But no Griefing - We allow stealing valuable blocks (Diamond, Lapis, Emerald, etc), but we enforce no griefing. Just because you found a house in the wilderness unprotected, doesn't mean you can take it apart. If you find chests within, though, the loot is all yours.

  • We got Story - Despite all the brutality Loka can offer, the server also has a rich backstory, RP events, and uncommon oddities that occur in the world. If you like to be involved in the story, Loka is your place.

  • Competitive much? - Loka hosts a number of pvp scenarios if you like to whet your appetite in a competitive arena or battleground. We take our PvP seriously too from ELO-rated Arenas to end-of-season rewards including titles and custom horses. Show us what you got.
